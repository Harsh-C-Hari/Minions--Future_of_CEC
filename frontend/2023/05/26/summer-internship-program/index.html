SUMMER INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

  • May 26, 2023
  • Posted by: Author Anil
  • Category: Academics
  • Comments Off on SUMMER INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

The 5-day internship program at the College of Engineering Chengannur! This program is designed to inspire and motivate higher secondary students to pursue novel ideas and explore technological possibilities outside the scope of their usual academic curriculum through project-based learning. Our goal is to provide students with hands-on experience with real projects that will help them prepare for college and develop valuable technical skills.

Through this program, students will have the opportunity to expand their technical expertise and delve into the endless possibilities of science, technology, and social development. Our one-of-a-kind internship will allow students to earn while they learn and develop practical skills that can benefit them in the future. By participating in this program, students will be aligning themselves with the “Digital India, the power to empower” philosophy of using digital technology to empower individuals. Our challenging and informative internship provides a rewarding experience that fosters learning and personal growth. If you have a passion for technology, this is the perfect opportunity to explore your interests and expand your horizons.

Program Objectives:
Motivate youth to discover and nurture the creative side hidden in them.

Help youth in choosing their careers.

To inspire students with hands-on experience in emerging technologies.

Encourage youth to be more productive.

Enable students to earn income and gain practical work experience during their learning
