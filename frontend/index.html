College at a glance

An engineering education rooted in Kerala, open to the world.

The College of Engineering Chengannur (CEC) was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the IHRD in 1993. Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), CEC has maintained its standing as a leading engineering college in Kerala. Located in Chengannur town, Alappuzha district, the campus has excellent access to transport, communication, and lodging.
