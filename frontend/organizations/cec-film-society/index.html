CEC Film Society

“Cinema is a matter of what’s in the frame and what’s out.”

Film Society is the platform for all the students who are passionate about different fields of filmmaking, where they can learn and progress in the art of filmmaking. The Inaugural Film Society of College of Engineering Chengannur was by the famous Cine artist Antony Varghese(Pepe) in the inaugural function of the college Arts Festival in the year 2018-19. 

It is an art-loving platform to promote various film-based activities. CEC Film Society has been part of The World’s Largest Filmmaking Challenge by being in the 50-hour filmmaking challenge by the India Film Project(IFP). 

We succeeded in the challenge by making a short film duration of 6 minutes. We are planning to make short films and organize TedX talks with professional filmmakers, Film-based competitions, and workshops on Editing, screenwriting, and other aspects of filmmaking.
